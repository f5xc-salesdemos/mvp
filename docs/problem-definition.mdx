sidebar:

  label: Problem & Opportunity
---

- F5 Distributed Cloud practitioners spend disproportionate time on repetitive knowledge gathering and artifact production, displacing the high-leverage work only humans can do.
- Workflows are artisanal: each individual reinvents their own approach, producing inconsistent quality and no shared institutional memory.
- The opportunity is to amplify human capacity — not replace it — by offloading recurring knowledge work to an agentic subject matter expert.
